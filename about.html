About
hello & welcome
I’m Carrie Koehmstedt, the owner & designer of Carrie Loves Design Studio.

I started out blogging on a Blogspot blog in the Spring of 2006 (Carrie & the Koehmstedts) as as a way to keep in touch with my family in California. I quickly began designing and re-designing my own blog and in the process discovered I really enjoyed the creative process of customizing a website. I have always had a creative soul but never would have thought that I would call myself a website designer, be running my own business, and working with wonderfully talented people from all corners of the world!

I am so happy I get to do something that I truly enjoy – helping authors, creatives, and self-starters like yourself make your slice of the internet look & feel as special as you!
